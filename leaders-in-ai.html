Leaders in AI

The researchers, founders, and builders shaping artificial intelligence. Follow these people and you'll always know what matters — before it makes the news.

10 leaders Researchers & Builders Free to follow
👤
Sam Altman
CEO · OpenAI
Sam Altman
The person most responsible for putting AI in everyone's hands. As CEO of OpenAI, Altman shaped the release of ChatGPT, GPT-4, and the broader push toward AGI. His posts and interviews offer an unfiltered view of where the frontier is heading.
Free Blog · Twitter/X CEO All levels
👤
Andrej Karpathy
AI Educator · ex-OpenAI, Tesla
Andrej Karpathy
The best AI educator alive. Former head of AI at Tesla and founding member of OpenAI, Karpathy now creates free educational content that makes deep learning genuinely understandable. His "Neural Networks: Zero to Hero" series is a must-watch.
Free Substack · YouTube Educator All levels
👤
Yann LeCun
Chief AI Scientist · Meta
Yann LeCun
Turing Award winner and one of the original deep learning pioneers. As Meta's Chief AI Scientist, LeCun is a vocal critic of the AGI hype cycle and a proponent of open-source AI. Opinionated, rigorous, and always worth reading — even when you disagree.
Free Twitter/X Researcher Intermediate+
👤
Demis Hassabis
CEO · Google DeepMind
Demis Hassabis
Nobel Prize winner and CEO of Google DeepMind. Hassabis led the team behind AlphaFold — arguably AI's most impactful scientific breakthrough — and continues to push the frontier on reasoning, science, and general intelligence at Google's largest AI lab.
Free Twitter/X Researcher · CEO All levels
👤
Geoffrey Hinton
Nobel Laureate · "Godfather of AI"
Geoffrey Hinton
The godfather of deep learning and 2024 Nobel Prize laureate in Physics. Hinton's foundational work on neural networks underpins virtually every modern AI system. He left Google in 2023 to speak freely about AI risks — a voice that carries unique weight in the field.
Free Academic · Interviews Researcher All levels
👤
Ilya Sutskever
CEO · Safe Superintelligence
Ilya Sutskever
Co-founder of OpenAI and one of the most influential researchers in modern deep learning. After departing OpenAI, Sutskever founded Safe Superintelligence (SSI) with a singular mission: build safe superintelligence. Few people have had more influence on the course of AI development.
Free ssi.inc · Twitter/X Researcher · Founder Intermediate+
👤
Andrew Ng
Founder · deeplearning.ai · Coursera
Andrew Ng
The person who made AI education accessible to millions. Founder of deeplearning.ai and Coursera's ML specialization, Ng has a unique talent for translating frontier AI into clear, actionable learning. His "AI for Everyone" course is the best starting point for non-technical learners.
Free Newsletter · Twitter/X Educator · Founder All levels
👤
Fei-Fei Li
Co-Founder · World Labs · Stanford AI
Fei-Fei Li
Creator of ImageNet — the dataset that ignited the deep learning revolution. Li is a Stanford professor, former Google Cloud Chief Scientist, and co-founder of World Labs, focused on spatial intelligence. A leading voice on both the potential and human responsibility of AI.
Free Substack · Twitter/X Researcher · Founder All levels
👤
Dario Amodei
CEO · Anthropic
Dario Amodei
Co-founder and CEO of Anthropic, the AI safety company behind Claude. Amodei's essay "Machines of Loving Grace" is one of the most compelling cases for AI's positive potential. A deeply thoughtful voice on both capability and safety — rare in an industry that often treats them as opposites.
Free Blog · Essays CEO · Safety All levels
👤
Mustafa Suleyman
CEO · Microsoft AI
Mustafa Suleyman
Co-founder of DeepMind (now Google DeepMind) and CEO of Microsoft AI. Suleyman's book "The Coming Wave" is essential reading for understanding where AI and biotech are headed — and why containment may be the defining challenge of our era. A builder and a thinker in equal measure.
Free Blog · Twitter/X CEO · Founder All levels
