Leaders in AI

The researchers, founders, and builders shaping AI. Follow these people and you'll always know what matters — before it becomes mainstream.

Andrej Karpathy

Independent Researcher & Educator

The best explainer of how AI works from first principles. Deep technical clarity with rare accessibility — his YouTube lectures and posts are required watching for anyone serious about understanding LLMs.

X @karpathy karpathy.ai YouTube

Dario Amodei

CEO, Anthropic

Leads the lab building Claude. His essays on AI safety and economic impact are among the most substantive writing on what AI actually means for the world — not hype, not doom, just clear thinking.

X @DarioAmodei Anthropic

Demis Hassabis

CEO, Google DeepMind

Built AlphaFold and AlphaGo. His track record on long-term research bets — things that seemed impossible until they weren't — is unmatched in the industry.

X @demishassabis DeepMind

Sam Altman

CEO, OpenAI

Sets the public narrative around AI capability and deployment timelines more than anyone else in the industry. Influential and worth watching closely — both for what he says and what he doesn't.

X @sama OpenAI

Yann LeCun

Chief AI Scientist, Meta AI

Pioneer of convolutional networks. Frequent, opinionated critic of current LLM approaches — a necessary counterpoint in an industry prone to groupthink. Follow him for the debate, not just the consensus.

X @ylecun Meta AI

Geoffrey Hinton

Independent Researcher · Nobel Laureate

Godfather of deep learning. Left Google in 2023 to speak freely about AI risk. His concerns about where this technology is headed carry unusual weight — he helped build it.

X @geoffreyhinton Website

Fei-Fei Li

Co-Director, Stanford HAI · Founder, World Labs

Created ImageNet, which catalyzed the deep learning revolution. Now building World Labs focused on spatial intelligence. The leading voice on human-centered AI at the frontier.

X @drfeifei World Labs

Ilya Sutskever

Co-founder & CEO, Safe Superintelligence Inc.

Co-invented key techniques behind GPT-3 and beyond. Now building SSI, focused entirely on safe superintelligence with no distractions. Rare conviction, rare technical depth.

X @ilyasut SSI

Emad Mostaque

Independent · AI Sovereignty Advocate

Founded Stability AI and shaped the open-source AI movement. Now an independent voice on AI sovereignty and national AI strategy. One of the most unconventional thinkers in the space.

X @EMostaque

Nat Friedman & Daniel Gross

AI Grant

Running the most influential AI research grant program. Their portfolio reflects where serious builders are actually going — not where the hype cycle is pointing. Two of the sharpest allocators in AI.

X @natfriedman X @danielgross AI Grant
